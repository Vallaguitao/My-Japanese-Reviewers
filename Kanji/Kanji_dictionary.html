N5 & N4 Foundation

Overview & Progress

Mastering Kanji is essential for Japanese fluency. The JLPT N5 requires knowledge of approximately 100 basic characters, focusing on numbers, days, and simple concepts. The JLPT N4 expands this by roughly 300 additional characters, covering daily life, family, and basic actions.

Distribution of characters required per level.

How to use this tool

  • • Use the Search bar to find specific meanings or readings.
  • • Click the Level Filters to isolate N5 or N4 kanji.
  • • Click any Kanji card to view its Onyomi, Kunyomi, and common vocabulary examples.
