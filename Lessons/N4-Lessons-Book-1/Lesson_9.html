Grammar 1

Nで (手段しゅだん)

Means / Method
📝 Example
アニメで日本語にほんごを勉強べんきょうしました。
I studied Japanese by watching anime.

📖 How It Works

You learned で to indicate place (学校がっこうで = at school) in earlier lessons. Here, で also indicates the means or method of doing something. In this lesson, we use it to describe how you studied Japanese.

📍 で = Place
学校がっこうで勉強べんきょうしました。
I studied at school.
🔧 で = Means/Method
アプリで勉強べんきょうしました。
I studied with an app.

💬 Conversation Example
A:どこで日本語にほんごを勉強べんきょうしましたか？
Where did you study Japanese?
B:日本語学校にほんごがっこうで勉強べんきょうしました。

C:私わたしはネットで勉強べんきょうしました。
I studied on the Internet.
💡 To Ask About Method Specifically:
Use どうやって (how) instead of どこで (where):

「どうやって日本語にほんごを勉強べんきょうしましたか？」= "How did you study Japanese?"

Grammar 2

N1はN2が ナA/イA

Describing Specific Characteristics
📝 Example
日本語にほんごは文字もじが難むずかしいです。
The characters are a difficult part of Japanese.

📖 How It Works

This expression clarifies which specific element of something has a certain characteristic. Instead of simply saying "Japanese is difficult," you specify what part of Japanese is difficult (characters, grammar, pronunciation, etc.).

📐 Structure
N1は + N2が + ナA-です / イA-いです

N1 = topic (e.g., 日本語にほんご) | N2 = specific element (e.g., 文字もじ, 文法ぶんぽう, 発音はつおん)
When N2 is a verb, add の: N1は V-るの が〜

🟡 N1 = Topic
日本語にほんごは…
英語えいごは…
タイ語ごは…
💜 N2 = Specific Element
…文字もじが (characters)
…文法ぶんぽうが (grammar)
…発音はつおんが (pronunciation)
…読よむのが (reading)
💬 More Examples
日本語にほんごは話はなすのが大変たいへんです。
Speaking Japanese is hard.
英語えいごは文法ぶんぽうが簡単かんたんです。
English grammar is easy.
タイ語ごは文字もじを覚おぼえるのが難むずかしいです。
Memorizing Thai characters is a difficult part of learning Thai.
💡 When N2 is a Verb:
Add の to nominalize the verb:

話はなすのが難むずかしい = Speaking is difficult
読よむのが簡単かんたん = Reading is easy

Grammar 3

N1は〜。(でも、) N2は〜。

Comparison (対比たいひ)
📝 Example
日本語にほんごは、読よむのは少すこし難むずかしいです。でも、会話かいわは得意とくいです。
Reading Japanese is a bit difficult. But I am good at speaking in Japanese.

📖 How It Works

This expression compares two aspects of something. The particle は is used to mark both items being contrasted. When comparing, が changes to は to emphasize the contrast.

📐 Structure
N1は〜。(でも、) N2は〜。

When contrasting, が → は to emphasize the comparison
でも adds emphasis to the contrast but can be omitted

🔸 Without Comparison
日本語にほんごは、読よむのが難むずかしいです。
Reading Japanese is difficult.
🔹 With Comparison
読よむのは難むずかしいです。でも、会話かいわは得意とくいです。
Reading is difficult, but conversation I'm good at.
💬 More Examples
ひらがなは好すきです。カタカナは苦手にがてです。
I like hiragana, but I am not good at katakana.
英語えいごは文法ぶんぽうが簡単かんたんです。でも、単語たんごを覚おぼえるのは大変たいへんです。
English grammar is easy, but it is difficult to memorize words.
💡 Key Point:
When contrasting two things, both items use は (not が) to emphasize the comparison. The second は implies "but as for THIS..."

Key Point: MNN explains that は marks topics, but when two things are being compared, は emphasizes each item in the contrast. This contrastive は is one of the most important uses of the particle.

Grammar 4

V-てもらえませんか？

Polite Requests
📝 Example
日本語にほんごをチェックしてもらえませんか？
Will you check my Japanese?

📖 How It Works

This expression is used to politely request something from another person. While V-てください can be used for requests, V-てもらえませんか is a more polite way of asking.

📐 Structure
V-て + もらえませんか？

もらう = to receive (a favor) | もらえませんか = "could you (do this for me)?"
Literally: "Could I receive the favor of you doing V?"

⚪ Standard: V-てください
教おしえてください。
Please teach me.
🟢 More Polite: V-てもらえませんか
教おしえてもらえませんか？
Could you teach me?
💬 Common Request Verbs
この漢字かんじの読よみ方かたを教おしえてもらえませんか？
Will you teach me how to read this kanji character?
新あたらしい教科書きょうかしょ、ちょっと見みせてもらえませんか？
Will you quickly show me a new textbook?
💡 Useful Verbs with てもらえませんか:

教おしえて (teach)  |  見みせて (show)  |  貸かして (lend/borrow)
説明せつめいして (explain)  |  書かいて (write)  |  言いって (say)
話はなして (talk/speak)

Grammar 5

V-方かた

"How to do ~"
📝 Example
この書類しょるいの書かき方かたがよくわかりません。
I do not know how to fill out this paperwork.

📖 How It Works

〜方かた indicates "how to do ~" or "the way of doing ~". Attach 方かた to the masu-stem of the verb (remove ます, add 方かた).

📐 Structure
V-ます → V-方かた

書かきます → 書かき方かた (how to write)
読よみます → 読よみ方かた (how to read)

読よみ方かた
how to read
書かき方かた
how to write
し方かた
how to do
やり方かた
how to do (casual)
行いき方かた
how to go
使つかい方かた
how to use
食たべ方かた
how to eat
作つくり方かた
how to make
💬 More Examples
日本語にほんごのいい練習れんしゅうのし方かたがありますか？
Do you have tips on how to practice Japanese effectively?
この辞書じしょの使つかい方かたを教おしえてください。
Will you teach me how to use this dictionary?
💡 Combining with てもらえませんか:

「この漢字かんじの読よみ方かたを教おしえてもらえませんか？」
= "Could you teach me how to read this kanji?"

This combines Grammar 4 and 5 perfectly!
