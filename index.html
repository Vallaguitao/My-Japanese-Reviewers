Recommended study path

A simple flow for learners who want structure instead of choosing lessons randomly.

N5 BasicsStart with greetings, particles, and simple sentence patterns.
DescriptionsStudy adjectives, comparison, counters, and daily vocabulary.
Verb FormsFocus on て-form, ない-form, dictionary form, and た-form.
N4 ExpansionMove to longer expressions, reasons, rules, plans, and conversations.
Vocabulary + KanjiBuild recognition, pronunciation, and example sentence practice.
今日の一歩: Choose one lesson, write three examples, and say them aloud. Every small review counts.
