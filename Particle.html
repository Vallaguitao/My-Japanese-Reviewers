Digital Cheat Sheet (Irodori A2)

1 Giving & Receiving (L1-4)

あげます

I give to someone. (or someone gives to someone else).

もらいます

I receive from someone (Source marked by に or から).

くれます

Someone gives to ME (or my in-group).

2 Crucial Forms & Expressions

  • •
    〜た ほうがいいです: You should do... (Giving Advice). Uses Ta-form.
  • •
    〜ないで ください: Please do not... Uses Nai-form.
  • •
    〜ても いいですか: May I...? (Asking permission). Uses Te-form.
  • •
    〜て しまいました: Did something completely OR unfortunately.
  • •
    〜んです: Used to explain a reason or ask for an explanation. (Casual: ~n da).

3 Conditionals

〜たら (Tara) If / When

Ta-form + ら (e.g., 行[い]ったら). Covers most "If/When" situations in A2.

〜ばいいですか Asking for instructions

"What should I do?" / "How should I go?" e.g., どうやって行[い]けばいいですか / 行[い]ったらいいですか.
